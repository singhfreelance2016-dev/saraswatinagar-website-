About Us

Saraswatinagar Institute for Excellence, established with a vision to provide world-class educational opportunities, has become a trusted name in Kathmandu. Located in Bhrikuti Chowk near Apex Life School, we specialize in language training, test preparation, and comprehensive study abroad counseling.

Our dedicated team of expert instructors and counselors has successfully guided over 500 students to secure visas and admissions in top destinations including the UK, Japan, Australia, and Korea. With a commitment to maintaining a 100% COE success rate, we offer both online and physical classes to provide flexible learning pathways for every student. At Saraswatinagar Institute, we don't just teach; we open minds and create futures.
